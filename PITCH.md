## üí° Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: "We use Gemini Pro with carefully crafted prompts. In testing, it achieves 90%+ accuracy on action item extraction. Plus, confidence scores flag uncertain items for review."

**Q: What if the AI gets it wrong?**
A: "Every task is editable. Users can quickly fix any mistakes. The AI is an assistant, not a replacement for human judgment."

**Q: Can it integrate with existing tools?**
A: "Absolutely! The architecture supports Slack notifications, Gmail integration, and calendar sync. We focused on core functionality for the hackathon, but these are straightforward additions."

**Q: How does it handle privacy?**
A: "All processing happens on your server. We don't send data to third parties except Gemini for AI processing. You control your data."

**Q: What's the tech stack?**
A: "Next.js frontend, Express backend, Gemini Pro for AI, and file-based storage that's easily upgradable to Firestore or MongoDB."

**Q: How long did this take to build?**
A: "The core MVP was built in one intensive day, proving the concept is both powerful and achievable."
